About

Hi! I'm Emily.

Thanks for checking out my work. Like a lot of people in UX, I didn't start here. I took a few creative detours along the way, and I'm glad I did.

These days I'm at Fidelity Investments, working on tools for people dealing with student debt. I like getting into complex problems and finding ways to make them feel a little clearer and easier to navigate.

Outside of work, I try to balance out the screen time. My dog, Banjo, gets me outside and moving. Yoga helps me slow down. And when I need a break from thinking, I'll pick up clay or paint, just making things for the sake of it.

It's all part of what keeps me grounded, curious, and ready to take on the next challenge.
